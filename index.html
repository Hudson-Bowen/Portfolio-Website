About Me

Hello, My name is Hudson Bowen, and I'm a second-year Electrical and Electronics Engineering major at the University of Georgia. I'm a member of UGA Motorsports, working on the grounded low-voltage system on the Formula Electric team. After college, I plan on working in automation or motorsports.

Current Projects

Formula Electric Grounded Low Voltage Circuitry

Academic Project

As a member of the UGA Motorsport Formula Electric team, I am part of a group tasked with creating the grounded low-voltage system. This system contains basic driver controls and a safety circuit that shuts the car down if sensors detect a fault. We are in the early stages of development and will continue working to have it ready for our first competition.

Future Projects

Remote Camera Birdhouse

For this project, I want to make a birdhouse with a camera that records when birds enter or exit. The camera would look at the entrance and record the birds' movement, then send these clips to a computer for viewing.
